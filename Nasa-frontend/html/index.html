Every day, our planet is bombarded by tons of cosmic dust and small meteoroids.
But occasionally, larger objects cross Earth's path - objects that could cause regional or global devastation.
Our mission is to detect these threats early and develop strategies to protect our world.
Using data from NASA's Near-Earth Object Program and advanced simulation technologies,
We can predict impact scenarios and test deflection methods before it's too late.
The future of planetary defense begins here.

Advanced Technologies

Kinetic Impactors

Deploy spacecraft to collide with asteroids and alter their trajectories. Calculate required mass, velocity, and impact timing for successful deflection missions.

Gravity Tractors

Use spacecraft gravity to gradually pull asteroids off collision courses. Simulate long-term orbital changes through gravitational attraction over extended periods.

Nuclear Options

Evaluate nuclear deflection scenarios for large or short-warning threats. Model standoff detonations and subsurface explosions for maximum effect.
